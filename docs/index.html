Welcome to the UCLA Biostatistics Student Association!

The Biostatistics Student Association is a student group whose mission is to create an environment conducive to academic growth, professional development, networking, and socializing among UCLA biostatistics students. We work to represent the needs and concerns of the student body and facilitate conversations between students and faculty within the department.
